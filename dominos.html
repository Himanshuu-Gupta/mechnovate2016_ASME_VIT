Dominos


The workshop aims at providing technical knowledge about the use of modern weapon technology. Mainly focusing on weaponry techniques theworkshop shall cover the basics of infantry weapon designing andmanufacturing. Furthermore the participants will get to know aboutvarious missile systems and their capabilities.


Course Content:
    o Designing of infantry weapons.
    o Various missile systems and their capabilities.
    o Discussion on Nuclear and Thermo-nuclear Weapons.

Judging Criteria

    o The best design (or) arrangement of the chips.
    o No. of times the bell is hit.

Rules and Regulations

    o Only the materials provided can be used for the setup.
    o Disturbing the setup of other team will result in disqualification.

Eligibility

  • o The participants can take part in teams consisting of a maximum of 3 members or even individually if they so wish.
  • o Students pursuing either an Undergraduate or Postgraduate degree are eligible.

Details

  • Date: 19th March
  • Timings: 8:30AM - 6:00PM
  • Duration: 8 hours
  • Venue: MB 220
  • Fees: Rs 100
