Weapon Technology Workshop


The workshop aims at providing technical knowledge about the use of modern weapon technology. Mainly focusing on weaponry techniques theworkshop shall cover the basics of infantry weapon designing andmanufacturing. Furthermore the participants will get to know aboutvarious missile systems and their capabilities.


Course Content:
    o Designing of infantry weapons.
    o Various missile systems and their capabilities.
    o Discussion on Nuclear and Thermo-nuclear Weapons.

Eligibility

  • Students and Research Scholars
  • Professional Consultants and Practicing Engineers
  • Faculties

Details

  • Timings: 9:30AM - 6:00PM
  • Duration: 6 hours
  • Venue: TT Gallery II
