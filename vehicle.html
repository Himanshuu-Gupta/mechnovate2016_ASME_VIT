VEHICLE RECONSTRUCTING Workshop


Vehicle Overhauling Workshop offers a detailed session on design, manufacturing, dismantling and modifications of a vehicle. It will give the participants an immense grasp on Automobile Systems and will offer a pragmatic experience to the participants, focusing on mastering their skills through hands-on sessions.The workshop will focus on major vehicular components, vehicle layout, categorization, and body design like trims, fenders, noise harness and other related topics.

Course Content:
    o Engine Dismantling: This includes starting systems, intake and exhaust manifolds, ignition systems, cooling and lubrication systems, exhaust systems and its norms.
    o Transmission Dismantling: This will include clutch requirements, types, specifications, transmission systems, gear selection ratios and power drives.
    o The Suspension, Steering and Brakes Session will give a brief focus on design, geometries, and assembly of suspension systems whilst laying an overview on vehicle alignment, steering Systems, hydraulic braking, ABS, DAC and ESP.

Eligibility

  • Students and Research Scholars
  • Professional Consultants and Practicing Engineers
  • Faculties
